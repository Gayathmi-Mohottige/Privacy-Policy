EleEYE – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2025

EleEYE (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the EleEYE mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the app:

  • Location Data: We collect your device's GPS location to provide real-time alerts and improve road safety.
  • Device Information: Basic device details like model, OS version, and push token to support notification services.
  • Community Reports: Submitted locations and notes are stored temporarily in our secure database.
  • Analytics (Optional): Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve performance and features.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Send real-time elephant warnings and map alerts
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Assist drivers and villagers with road safety awareness

3. Backend and Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud platform. All communication is encrypted using HTTPS.

4. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver real-time notifications based on your location and community reports.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is used only within the EleEYE system to enhance road safety.

6. Data Retention

Community reports are stored for 30 minutes and then automatically removed. Other system logs may be temporarily kept for analysis.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from users in this age group.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Major changes will be announced in the app or through an updated version of this page.
